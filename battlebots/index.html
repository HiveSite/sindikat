About the Project

BattleBots.me is a national STEAM competition (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) for young people aged 15–30. Teams design, program, and build combat robots that face off in a live arena.

The project is supported by Spark.me STEAM initiative and aims to inspire innovation, teamwork, and practical engineering skills among Montenegro's youth.

This groundbreaking initiative combines education with entertainment, creating an engaging platform where theoretical knowledge meets practical application.

How It Works

1

Education Bootcamp

Workshops in 5 cities covering robotics, Arduino, CAD, sensors, and programming fundamentals.

  • • Arduino programming & electronics
  • • 3D CAD design & modeling
  • • Sensor integration & control systems
  • • Robot mechanics & movement
  • • Team building & project management
2

Robot Construction

10 selected teams build robots using identical kits with dedicated mentor support.

  • • 8-week construction period
  • • Weekly mentor check-ins
  • • Access to fabrication labs
  • • Safety training & protocols
  • • Testing & iteration cycles
3

Live Arena

Spectacular final battle in a custom-built arena, judged by experts and streamed online.

  • • Tournament-style elimination
  • • Live streaming & commentary
  • • Expert judging panel
  • • Awards ceremony
  • • Media coverage & interviews
